MY   LIFE  JOURNEY
  

  • Birth Name :  Chanakya

  • Commonly Known As :   Raj. Vishnu Kumar


  • Born :   January 23, 1999

  • Birth Place :   Coimbatore (Tamil Nadu),  India


  • Height :  170 cm

  • Weight :  65 - 75 kg


  • Native Language :  Tamil

  • Nick Names :   RVK, Vichu, Sagasakara



Introduction

I was born on January 23, 1999, in Coimbatore. My Father is a M.E and MBA graduate, who was an entrepreneur, and My Mother who has completed schooling, takes care of our lovely family and home. I grew up following my dad’s path, benefiting from his versatile knowledge, and my mom’s deep care. I am a person who loves nature, pets, cycling, and riding bikes for infinity.





My  Parents


        

My Father,  Mr. P. Raj Ratan Swamy,  was born in Assam and is of Tamil origin. He grew up and completed his schooling there since his father (my grandfather) led the heavy weight transportation and equipment department in ONGC, while his mother (my grandmother), who completed her entire schooling education independently and fully and also knows 4 languages completely, took care of the home. After his schooling, my dad returned to Tamil Nadu to pursue his college education. He enrolled at Hindusthan College of Engineering in Chennai.


After completing his first degree, he began working at Stanes Company in Coimbatore while simultaneously pursuing his master's degree. Upon completing his master's, he joined ELGI Company in Coimbatore and parallelly pursued an MBA. Following his MBA, he was promoted as manager within the same company and dedicated a decade to his work there. Eventually, he chose to leave and ventured into entrepreneurship, founding "Vishnu Kumar Engineering Enterprises," which he managed until his end. My dad is also proficient in languages, with knowledge of 8 languages, including 4 languages that he can read, write, and speak fluently.

In addition to his professional achievements, he excelled as a sports player across various games and served as a remarkable mentor to many, aiding them in achieving success in their lives. He also held certification as a film director and even had opportunities to act in movies. However, due to my grandparents' disapproval of the entertainment industry, he discontinued his involvement in films.


My Mother,  Mrs. R. Mani Megalai,  is a Coimbatore native person, born to parents where her father (my grandfather) has completed schooling and was a great politician, and her mother (my grandmother) took care of farming and domestic animals at home. She completed her entire schooling independently. She is talented in flower decoration, creating plastic wire baskets, tailoring, and teaching primary school students.
As my dad and mom were cousins, they fell in love with each other, and with the acceptance of our grandparents, they got married.





Childhood  And  School  Life

I began speaking at the age of 4, but before that, my parents worried that I lacked speaking ability. They took me to various medical assessments and doctors, thinking it was a natural inability. This concern also affected my school admission. Initially, many schools rejected me because I was quiet. After a persistent effort, I finally embarked on my school journey at G.R.G. Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Coimbatore.



From 2003 to 2017, I completed my entire 14-years of education in that very school, a place that felt like heaven where I gained many lifelong friendships. It became a second home to me, offering not just knowledge but also invaluable experiences of various emotions and situations.


While I wasn't particularly apt toward learning from textbooks or formal teacher training, I found myself easily distracted by sports, the scenery outside my classroom window, and my friends. Yet, this never bothered me much because my father played multiple roles in my life – he was my best teacher, trainer, coach, business mentor, and most importantly,  "My  Precious  Best Friend  Of  My  Life ". He always took me along to our company, allowing me to immerse myself in business tasks and learn through hands-on experience. His guidance gifted me with tremendous knowledge and implanted in me good habits.


However, the happiness I experienced Didn’t last long. My father's sudden demise left me alone to tackle this outside world, making everything seem different and challenging without him. I encountered numerous obstacles during this period of loss, and the pain still remains as a forever wound in my heart. Despite these challenges, I managed to complete my schooling in 2017, achieving  422 / 500 marks  in 10th grade and  863 / 1200 marks  in 12th grade.





First  Job Experience  
After  Discontinuing  Next Level  Education

After finishing school, I couldn't continue my education due to financial constraints. So, I joined PSG Hospital in Coimbatore as a Pharmacy and Medical Record Assistant. I was the youngest employee there, surrounded by colleagues ranging from 5 to 50 years elders of me. It was my first exposure to the real world after a school life which is focused on morals rather than the harsh realities outside. Despite this, I made many friends and became well-liked for my kindness and friendliness towards everyone, regardless of their education or job status.


However, as I gained recognition, some became jealous and turned against me. As a newcomer to the real world, I struggled to navigate this environment. It became clear that despite knowledge and kindness, one's influence and words are often disregarded without a basic college degree. Realizing this, I decided to pursue further education.


College  Life

After experiencing the real world but before completing my education, I resumed my second level of education at Hindusthan College of Arts and Science in Coimbatore. However, I found the knowledge gained there to be relatively low in comparison to my school experience. Although I made good college mates, I was unlucky not to form lifelong friendships. Unfortunately, my college life was cut short to just one and a half years due to the global pandemic – the coronavirus, which disrupted life worldwide, including my education.

The latter half of my college experience remains a blank space as I was unable to attend live classes on campus. Instead, it transitioned fully to online classes, which I mostly didn't attend due to a lack of facilities to connect. Overall, my college life was simple and uneventful, lacking the excitement or thrill that could have made a significant impact in my life.





Renewed  Job Career


Having already gained job experience without a respectable educational qualification, I decided to strengthen my career prospects by obtaining a college degree. I graduated with a B.Com CA degree. However, as soon as I completed my education, the weight of family responsibilities shifted onto my shoulders. I became the sole caretaker of my mom and the primary source of income for my family, a reality that hit me hard.


Despite having dreams, wishes, and passions for my life journey, I set them aside and chose the path of responsibility. It wasn't a decision made wholeheartedly, but rather out of a sense of loyalty to my life's circumstances. My focus shifted entirely to stabilizing my family's situation. After accomplishing that goal, I plan to revive my passions, dreams, and wishes. Until then, my focus remains on stabilizing my family's situation.




THE   JOURNEY   CONTINUES . . . . . . . . . .








  
MY  LIFE  JOURNEY'S  TIMELINE
  




Birth  of  an  Adventurer :  RVK's  Journey  Begins

On the wonderful evening of January 23, 1999, my mom introduced me to this exciting and thrilling world. My dad gathered me in his hands and welcomed me, promising to take care of me. Thus began  The Journey of  RVK,  The Adventurer of Life.

Stepping  into  Adventure

As the days passed, starting from the  End of 2000 , I began to walk on my own feet, marking the beginning of  My Adventurous Exploration  of this world with the support of my dad.

Breaking Silence :  A  New  Era  of  Communication  Begins

Until late 2002, I had remained silent, except for saying the word "Appa". This prolonged period of silence had worried my parents, leading to medical assessments and consultations. However, in Late 2002, I began to speak, marking the start of a New Era of Communication.

The  School  Journey  Begins

As I started speaking very late, it affected my school admission process, with many schools rejecting me due to my lack of ability to speak clearly. After overcoming numerous challenges, I finally joined G.R.G. Matriculation Higher Secondary School in June 2003. This marked the beginning of my educational journey and exploration of the outside world after a long period of being protected and confined to home. I didn't realize back then that it would be the place where I'd make lifelong friends and have amazing emotional experiences.

Computer's  Introduction  in  My  Life

In 2005, My dad bought a New Mercury Brand Computer, which was a big luxurious things in those days and introduced it to me by playing Tom and Jerry cartoons, NBA basketball games, and Need for Speed (NFS) games on it. This sparked my excitement and ignited my passion to learn and use computers.

First  Achievement  in  Art  and  Craft

On June 13, 2008, I achieved My First Merit Award in a coloring contest. Being fond of drawing and coloring, I got the opportunity to showcase my skills in a competition held at my school and organized by Colorama, a company under Hindusthan Pencils Limited. I won the 1st Merit Award in my age category, which was a proud and exciting moment for me on that time.

First  Drawing  Published  in  the  Thinathanthi  Newspaper

On  November 10, 2008 , Thinathanthi Newspaper selected and published my drawing featuring Dora and Bhujji, marking a proud moment in my passion for drawing.

Securing  an  A  Grade  in  the  Aptitude  Test

On  March 25, 2010 , I secured an  A grade  in the  Indira Gandhi National Open University Learning Aptitude Test , held at my school. This achievement also earned me a scholarship from  Adoro Multimedia Institution , recognizing and rewarding my performance.

Entered  into  My  Dream  of  Animation,  Game Developing  and  Designing

In  April  to  May  2010 , my dream of animation, game development and designing became a reality as I received a scholarship from  Adoro Multimedia.  Driven by my passion for gaming and animations, I eagerly joined the institution during summer vacation. There, I learned the basics of animation, including Adobe Photoshop CS3 and Adobe Flash software, and how to create basic animations. My dedication and progress soon earned recognition from the trainers, making me a standout student at the institution.

My  Best  Friend  -  My  Cycle ,  Enters  My  Life

On  January 17, 2011,  my dad fulfilled one of my excited wishes. We attended an expo at  Padmavathi Kalyana Mandabam in Hope College, Coimbatore.  To my surprise and delight, he gifted me a  Hercules Matrix Cycle  in advance for my birthday. This made me incredibly happy, especially since I had been asking for a cycle persistently.

Celebration  of  India's  World  Cup  Victory  with  My  Parents

On  April 2,  2011,    India's victory over Sri Lanka in the World Cup   was not just a national celebration but also one of my happiest memories. I watched this historic moment with my parents, and the joy of witnessing India's win was amplified by the celebrations around us. From our balcony, we saw youngsters in our surroundings throttling their bikes, shouting, and celebrating the victory. It remains one of the most precious moments of my life.

Second  Level  of  My  Dream  of  Animation,  Game  Developing  and  Designing

In  April  to  May  2011 , I progressed to the second stage of my animation course at  Adoro Multimedia.  Here, I delved into learning software like Combustion, Maya, and 3D Max (basics), further expanding my skills and knowledge in animation and design.

Transforming  into  a  Sports  Player

On  December 21, 2011,  I was selected in  My School's Basketball Team,  a proud moment that unfolded in front of my dad. He was especially happy because, despite being shorter at the time, he was concerned that I might not get recognized. However, my impressive hand-eye coordination in handling the ball and my cool approach to the game led to my selection. This marked the beginning of my journey in competitive sports.

Loss  of  My  Forever  Best  Friend  –  My  Dad

On November 26, 2012, I experienced one of the most painful moments of my life as I lost my best friend, my dad. His absence left a void that could never be filled, and the world seemed like a different place without him. His support and encouragement had always been my source of happiness, and losing him meant losing a part of myself.

4 th  Prize  in  Camel  Drawing  Competition

In  2013,  I achieved a milestone by winning the  4th Prize  in a  Camel Drawing Competition  held at my school. The competition, organized by Camel Company, recognized my artistic talent in my age category.

Left  My  Palace

In  November 2013,  Due to the immense pain caused by my dad's loss, our family decided to move from our home to a house near my grandma's place in another location. Although the elders made this decision, I was not interested at all, but I had no choice. This transition was particularly painful because my home was like a palace to me, and I had never experienced such a situation before.

Return  to  the  Palace

In  Early 2014,  the experience of living in another house reminded us of the saying  "East or West, Home is The Best".  We eventually returned to our beloved home, which we consider our palace. It was a huge relief after going through difficulty in the new surroundings, which felt like a decade, even though it was just for a short period.

10th  Public  Exams

After facing many obstacles following the loss of my dad, I managed to reach the 10th grade. Then came the  SSLC Exam,  which took place from  March 9, 2015  -  April 10, 2015.  The exam schedule gave us a lot of time between each exam, which my friends and I utilized 90% of the time to play cricket. As I am a slow writer, I faced a challenge during exams where I couldn't attempt any paper for 100 marks. The time would run out before I could complete the full question. This made exams particularly tricky for me. anyhow, the exams got over and we felt proud for overcoming a big challenge.

10th  Public  Exams  Result

After enjoying the summer vacation following the challenging 10th grade, the results of the  10th Public Exams  were announced on  May 21, 2015.  Despite not being able to complete any exam for the full mark, I had expected low marks. However, to my surprise, I scored  422 marks out of 500.  I got admission in the same school for 11th and 12th grade where I had been studying since the beginning. I chose the  Computer with Arts Course  and continued my higher secondary education.

First  Bank  Account  Opened  for  Me

As a  90s kid,  having  My Own Bank Account  felt like a major achievement to me. On  June 12, 2015,  I opened my first bank account, which made me feel like I had reached adulthood and gave me an exciting feeling that's hard to describe.

12th  Public  Exams

As the 11th and 12th grades progressed, the  12th Public Exams  began from  March 2, 2017  -  March 21, 2017.  However, due to facing more issues and feeling mentally unsettled during these grades, my interest in studies faded, and I became bored. As a result, I completed the 12th exams more as a formality than with genuine enthusiasm.

Last  Day  of  School  Life

Even though I didn't feel much joy about completing the 11th and 12th grades, the Most Painful Moment came on  March 21, 2017,  which was my last day as a school student. It was also my last day at the school where I had studied for  14 years, including LKG and UKG.  This was a difficult moment leaving  My Second Home,  the place that filled my life with memories and friendships. saying goodbye to my friends was especially hard, knowing we couldn't travel together like we did in our school days. It's a painful memory that always strikes deep in my heart.

12th  Public  Exams  Result

After completing the 12th grade without much joy, the  12th Public Exam Results  were announced on  May 12, 2017.  I had secured  863 marks out of 1200.  However, due to ongoing issues and my family's financial situation, I had to make the difficult decision to quit pursuing further education.

First  Job  Experience

Facing ongoing issues and living a dull life for months, on  November 7, 2017,  I joined  PSG Hospital  as an  Assistant in the Pharmacy and Medical Record Department.  It was my first exposure to the real world outside of school, where I quickly realized that it wasn't all about the good things as school had taught me. It was a completely different and harsh environment. Despite making many friends, I learned that respect often depends more on educational qualifications than on having a good heart in this job and its surroundings. This realization led me to quit after working there for some short period.

Receiving  My  Driver  License

As a  90s kid,  having a bank account was incredibly exciting, and getting a  Driver's License  was twice as exciting. On  March 12, 2018,  I obtained my driver's license using my own earnings, which was a moment of huge pride as it was achieved through my own hard work and money.

First  Hill  Bike  Drive  with  Friends

On  May 11, 2018,  as it was my close friend's birthday, we suddenly planned a trip to  Coonoor  just to have tea and return. It was my first long drive and hill drive, especially with friends. The misty climate and beautiful nature made it a cherishing memory.

Beginning  of  College  Life

As I gained experience of the outside world and understood the importance of educational qualifications, along with taking a one-year gap, on  July 5, 2018,  my college life began at  Hindusthan College of Arts and Science. 

Colleges  Closed  Due  to  Coronavirus

Since December 2019, we had been hearing about the  Coronavirus Outbreak  in China. On   March 17, 2020,  all colleges in India were closed due to the rapid spread of the virus in the country.

First  Time  Ever  Facing  Lockdown  of  Entire  Country

When colleges closed due to the spread of the coronavirus, we enjoyed a week of relaxation. However, on  March 25, 2020,  the  Indian Government  announced a  lockdown  for entire nation due to the rapid spread of the virus. This was a completely new situation for both my generation and my parents' generation. Initially, it was challenging to adjust, but we eventually got used to it. My friends and I started spending time by making funny videos, playing cricket, volleyball, or football on the road, going for night walks, having tea together as a group, playing games like Mini Militia, and more.

Get  Infected  by  the  Coronavirus  and  Started  to  Realize  the  Symptoms

As we continued to face lockdowns and tough situations for two years, I also lost some of my close relatives to the coronavirus. Attending their funerals was difficult. On  May 28, 2021,  I got  infected by the Coronavirus  after attending one of My close relation's funeral and started to experience symptoms.

Tested  Positive  for  Coronavirus  Attack

After experiencing severe symptoms, I underwent a medical test called  RTPCR,  and on  June 1, 2021,  I tested positive for a coronavirus infection. This news shocked both me and my mom, and we were completely devastated. The medical treatment for coronavirus patients was expensive, starting at around 1.5 lakh rupees, which is huge money for us during that difficult time and I was affected by the delta variant of the virus. I rushed to hospitals and labs for a CT scan, facing a very difficult time. I also searched for hospitalization options late into the night, but due to the high number of cases nationally, there were either no available beds or no admission for me as I came alone to hospitals.

Hospitalized  for  Coronavirus  Treatment

After searching for hospital admission even late into the nights, on  June 3, 2021,  in  Tiruppur,  I finally got hospitalized with the help of my relations. I stayed in the hospital for 8 days until  June 10, 2021.  It was a terrible experience leaving my mom alone in lockdown in Coimbatore while I suffered heavily from COVID-19,  undergoing intensive medical treatments. These were some of the most difficult and worst days of my life.

First  Day  in  the  CDS  Job

After facing the challenges of the pandemic, I completed my college education in the  first half of July 2021.  Upon trying to secure a job, I was selected by  CAP Digisoft Solutions Private Limited  on  August 11, 2021,  as a  Data and Document Analyst.  I'm not used to interacting much with girls unless I feel comfortable with them, similar to how I am with my boys' gang. However, in my team, there were 95% girls and only 2 boys initially. Despite this, the comfort and support provided by those girls in the team turned them into good friends, with some becoming friends for life. It was a great feeling to gain friends and knowledge in that company, but from an employee's perspective, the experience with management was very disappointing after spending 14 months there.

Introduction  of  My  Another  Best  Friend  -  My  Bike

As a  90s Kid,  everything holds a special significance, just like when I bought  My First Bike, the  FZ v3 Dark Knight , on  January 5, 2022.  The feeling is indescribable. Being fond of two-wheeled vehicles, my bike quickly became one of my best companions, alongside my cycle. They both are my silent friends who accompany me wherever I want to explore, whether it's in nature or around the neighborhood.

Freelance  Content  Writing  Job  in  Sansys  Business  Solutions

After leaving my job at CAP Digisoft Solutions, on  December 08, 2022,  I entered into a deal as a  Freelance Content Writer  with  Sansys Business Solutions  agency. I worked there for just 4 months, during which I made happy and funny memories with my teammates and gained some lifelong friends. Overall, it was a very short time yet good experience.

First  Day  in  the  VTG  -  ZENfra  Job

As  ChatGPT's Introduction  led to a decline in business for the agency, prompting me to seek another job. I received interview calls, attended them, and was successfully selected in Virtual Tech Gurus Company. On  April 3, 2023,  I joined the  ZENfra Product Team  at  Virtual Tech Gurus Company  as a Business Analyst. I was thrilled about this opportunity, particularly because the office was situated in Coimbatore IT park. And the journey began from there. I went on to showcase my versatile skills for the product I was working on, gained new knowledge as I was pushed to face new tasks and processes, and made valuable friends and colleagues. After 18 months, on August 2024, I left ZENfra - VTG.

Rebirth  of  My  Dad's  Computer

In  2005,  my dad bought a computer, which remained idle for a decade after his passing. On  September 6, 2023,  using my own earnings and with memories of my dad, I revived it by installing a new CPU and making it operational again. It was an incredible feeling to work on that old model computer, which held many memories with my dad.

Bought  My  First  Laptop

On  November 26, 2023,  I fulfilled a long-standing wish from my wish list by purchasing a  New Laptop  to nurture my creativity. As a  90s Kid,  owning a laptop was a significant milestone I had been wishing for over the past 15 years. Finally having one was an incredibly exciting feel.

Successfully  Completed  25  Years  in  This  Difficult  World

On  January 23, 2024,  I successfully marked a  Quarter of a Century  living on this challenging world, navigating through numerous changes, pains, emotions, and situations. This milestone is incredibly meaningful to me, as it symbolizes my resilience in standing tall among the difficulties of life.

Rescued  an  Injured  Puppy  in  Coimbatore  Tidel  Park  Campus

I noticed a  Very Thin and Weak Puppy  at the Coimbatore Tidel Park Campus during my break time walks over three consecutive days. The puppy looked very sad and seemed extremely hungry. I fed him some biscuits and cakes, but soon realized that his  back legs  were  paralyzed.  I was deeply worried and couldn't bear seeing the puppy in such a condition.
On the third day (29-05-2024), as soon as I understood that the puppy was injured, unable to move, and suffering from hunger and thirst, I contacted animal rescuers. After being directed from one person to another, I finally reached a person named  Bala Krishnan,  who was ready to rescue the puppy. Coordinating with him, he arrived at the  Tidel Park campus  at  8 PM.  I showed him where the puppy was, and he took the puppy to the  Thangam Memorial Trust shelter  in  Tiruppur. 
I felt very happy and relieved knowing that I had helped a suffering soul find peace from severe pain, hunger, thirst, and bad weather.
